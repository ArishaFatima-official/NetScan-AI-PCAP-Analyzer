Security — Data Exfiltration Risk
🕵️ Oversized ICMP Packets (10 > 1024B, max 1442B)
Normal pings are 64–128 bytes. Oversized ICMP is used for ICMP tunneling (data exfiltration), covert channels, or Ping of Death attacks.
▶ HOW TO FIX / WHAT TO DO
  • →Block oversized ICMP: iptables -A INPUT -p icmp -m length --length 256: -j DROP
  • →Inspect payloads for base64/encoded data
  • →Use Snort/Zeek rules to detect ICMP tunneling tools
